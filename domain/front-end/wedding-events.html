Wedding & Events offers

Corbett the Grand is the ideal choice for your destination weddings in Jim Corbett. Weddings are beautiful with stunning landscapes, soothing weather, setting up a lush garden for your ceremony and a warm welcome.

Corbett Grand caters to all your needs with a 100 room accommodation, four spacious, air conditioned, versatile meeting and function venues, opening to pools and gardens, beautifully landscaped gardens, party catering to over 2000 people and reserves Gives lawn to do. . Indoor and outdoor areas for breakouts and catering.

Each venue in Corbett Grand boasts a natural essence. Inbuilt modern technology, the latest theme décor, professional friendly service and abundant free parking are available on site, making Corbett the most preferred and popular choice for large celebrations throughout the Corbett area.

Corbett Grand caters to a wide range of functions and events - be it a gala dinner, intimate gathering, wedding or anniversary. The largest hall less hall of 4200 square feet can seat 350 guests with a stage and dance floor. Our gardens provide an ideal place for your mehndi and teak ceremonies and receptions.

Our wedding packages are designed to make it easy for you to choose your theme and tailor for your personal taste. The benefit of planning your wedding at Corbett the Grand is that everything you need is here. Choosing Corbett Grand as your wedding venue, you can be absolutely confident to enjoy our expertise, experience, care and support in planning your event.
